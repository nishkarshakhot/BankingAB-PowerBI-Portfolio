# 📈 Business Insights

## Executive Summary

The dashboard provides an enterprise-level overview of banking operations including lending, deposits, profitability, customer transactions, and Non-Performing Assets (NPA).

---

## Key Insights

### Loan Portfolio

- Total loan disbursement increased across major branches.
- Retail loans contributed the highest share.
- Home loans generated the largest interest income.

---

### Deposit Analysis

- Fixed Deposits accounted for the majority of deposits.
- CASA deposits showed steady growth.

---

### NPA Analysis

- Certain branches reported higher NPA ratios.
- Most NPAs were concentrated in the 90+ DPD bucket.

---

### Profitability

- Interest income remained the primary revenue source.
- High-performing branches generated significantly higher profits.

---

## Recommendations

- Improve recovery efforts for high-NPA branches.
- Increase CASA deposit acquisition.
- Promote high-performing loan products.
- Monitor branch-level profitability regularly.
